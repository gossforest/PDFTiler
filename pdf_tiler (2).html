How to use the tiles

  • Print at 100% / Actual Size. Never "Fit to Page" — it kills the scale.
  • Measure the 1-inch reference bar in the bottom margin of any tile to verify the print didn't scale.
  • Trim along the dashed crop line on the top and right of each tile, then overlap onto the next.
  • Align using the corner crosses and edge midpoint marks. The overlap zone gives you wiggle room.
